Sonic Squirrels FIRST Robotics Team

Snohomish Washington, 2017 - Present

On the Glacier Peak High School Robotics Team, I have held many titles and been apart of many different subteams. My Freshman and Sophomore years I was apart of the Media subteam, and my Sophomore Year I was the media lead where I managed the social media presence of the team and introduced SEO Optimization to the team's website. Then my Junior and Senior years I was apart of the programming subteam where I helped to design and test the vision pipeline for our robot, and my Senior year I was given the title of Co-Lead of Programming. On the Programming subteam, we write code in VS Code and use GitHub to manage it. Our subteam has 8 members and an adult mentor that helps us solve problems. As a group, we write code for our robots individual subsystems (parts ex: Drivetrain, intake, etc.) and create commands around those subsystems. We also write autonomous code for our robot.
