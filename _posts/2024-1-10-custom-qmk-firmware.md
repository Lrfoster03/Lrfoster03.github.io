So, over the past few years you could say that I've gotten into the realm of custom mechanical keyboards. I guess you could say I've gotten a lot into the realm of custom mechanical keyboards. So much so, that when my friend asked me to help him write the firmware for the specialized keyboard and PCB he was designing, I couldn't hesitate at the opportunity.

I introduce to you, The Compensator. A 40% monstrocity with a childishly goofy name, 10U Spacebar support, and a required 2 full keycap sets to fill it.

<img title="A 3D render of the Compensator Keyboard" alt="A 3D render of the Compensator Keyboard" src="/assets/images/compensator.png">


Crazy Right? The thing is laid out with an XT Column, Southpaw Numpad, Navigation Cluster, Alphas, Another Navigation Cluster, Numpad, and another XT Column. It's a lot to take in, but it's a lot of fun to type on.
